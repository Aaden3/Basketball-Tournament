Rules

🏀Official Rules & Guidelines 🏀

📏 Game Format

Team Size: 5-on-5 (with up to 3 subs per team)

Game Time: Two 15-minute halves, running clock ⏱️ Last 2 minutes of the second half: stoppage on whistles

Overtime: 2-minute overtime (if needed)

Double OT = sudden death (first team to score)

🎯 Scoring & Fouls

Scoring: Standard 2s and 3s

Fouls: 5 fouls per player = foul out

Team fouls = bonus after 7

Shooting fouls = free throws (1 for 2 pts or 1 for 3 pts unless otherwise specified)

Technical Fouls: 2 techs = ejection

Unsportsmanlike conduct will not be tolerated
